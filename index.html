About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, working with Prof. Long Hu. I received my Ph.D. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Min Chen (IEEE Fellow), and my bachelor's degree from Lanzhou University.

My research is driven by a simple question: how can machines better understand humans? To approach this question, I work on multimodal learning, large language models, and affective computing, with a focus on modeling human emotions, mental states, and behaviors from diverse sources of information.

I am especially interested in developing AI systems that are not only powerful, but also sensitive to human context. My current research explores multimodal foundation models, emotion-aware intelligence, and AI-driven methods for mental health and digital healthcare.

🌈 I am always open to collaboration on multimodal AI, affective computing, large language models, and AI for health. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in building human-centered AI together!
